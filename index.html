Our Story

About The Park Bakehouse

Baking with Tradition,
Inspired by Nature

The Park Bakehouse was born from a deep love of traditional breadmaking and a desire to bring genuinely great artisan bread to our community. Nestled next to a beautiful conservation area, our bakery reflects its surroundings — natural, unhurried, and full of character.

Our small, highly skilled team of bakers and pastry chefs arrive before sunrise every morning to begin the day's bake. Every loaf is made by hand on the premises, using time-honoured techniques passed down through generations of artisan bakers.

We adhere strictly to artisanal standards — no artificial additives, no preservatives, and no shortcuts. Just flour, water, salt, time, and genuine care.

Interior & Atmosphere

Contemporary
Scandinavian Style

Step inside and you'll find a calm, considered space with clean lines, warm natural materials, and a view over the conservation area beyond. We took inspiration from the Nordic tradition of hygge — warmth, simplicity, and the pleasure of good food shared with others.

Light woods, natural linen, handmade ceramics, and the scent of fresh bread in the air. A place to slow down.

What We Believe In

Ingredients & Ethos

The best bread starts with the best ingredients — and that means being honest, transparent, and uncompromising.

🌾 Organic Stoneground Flour

Certified organic stoneground flour retains all the grain's natural oils and nutrients, giving our bread a depth of flavour you simply cannot achieve with modern roller-milled flour.

🧫 Slow Fermentation

All our breads are naturally leavened using long, slow fermentation. Our sourdough cultures are years old and are the very heart of our bakery. Slow fermentation develops flavour and improves digestibility naturally.

🌿 Zero Additives

We use absolutely no artificial additives, preservatives, or improvers. Our ingredient lists are short and honest: flour, water, salt, and time. That's how great bread has always been made.

🪨 100% Rye Sourdough

Our pure rye loaf uses a 100% rye sourdough starter and organic rye flour from trusted mills. Rich in fibre, deeply flavoured, and wonderfully satisfying.

🧈 Finest Dairy

For our pastries, we source only the finest quality butter and dairy from farms we trust. Great pastry depends entirely on the quality of its butter — so we never compromise here.

♻️ Sustainability

We minimise packaging, compost all food waste, and work with local suppliers wherever possible. Our conservation area setting reminds us daily of our responsibility to the natural world.
